ABOUT US

The High School Leavers program was started with the aim of equipping christian teenagers and young adults with the right values before they enter into higher institution.

Launched in Zaria a city with a plethora of Universities and colleges, this program of birthed from a burden the ministry has about how ill-equipped many of our children area with challenges and temptation of life on campus and the ned to bridge that knowledge gap by providing relevant training in two areas.

The first focus of the team was the establishment on Bible studies, Personal Development, Prayers, Life on campus and Goal setting(to mention a few). This is to make them sound and non-compromising believers on campus.

At the same time, because of how difficult the economic climes of the country has become, our second area of focus is equipping them with a vocation they could practice on the side while in school to earn some income to support themselves in school, thereby protecting them from engaging in any vice in the desperation to raise money to fend fend for themselves.

This progam is faith-based and this means the children are accomodated, fed and generally catered for at no cost, except for some material for some of their practicals.
